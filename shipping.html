Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery with cold chain packaging for sensitive compounds.

Last updated: March 2026

Processing Times

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST, Monday through Friday, are processed and shipped the same business day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping: 2-3 business days. Free on orders over $100
  • Priority Shipping: 1-2 business days. $12.99
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day. $29.99

Shipping Area

PepLab.us currently ships to all 50 US states. We do not offer international shipping at this time.

Cold Chain Packaging

Temperature-sensitive products are shipped with gel ice packs and insulated packaging to maintain product integrity during transit. All peptides are shipped in sealed, light-protected vials.

Order Tracking

A tracking number is emailed to you once your order has shipped. You can use this number to track your package through the carrier's website.

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost, damaged, or significantly delayed, please contact us at contact@peplabs.us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue or send a replacement.

P.O. Boxes

We ship to P.O. boxes via USPS. Please note that expedited shipping options may not be available for P.O. box addresses.
