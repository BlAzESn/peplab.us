Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, and product quality.

All PepLab.us products are sold exclusively for in-vitro laboratory research and scientific study. They are not approved for human or veterinary use and are not intended for use as drugs, food additives, or cosmetics.
Every batch is tested by independent third-party laboratories using High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) for purity analysis and Mass Spectrometry (MS) for identity confirmation. We maintain a minimum purity standard of 99% and publish all Certificates of Analysis on our Test Results page.
All COAs are available on our Test Results page. You can review them before placing an order. Each COA includes HPLC chromatograms, mass spec data, and batch-specific purity percentages.

Temperature-sensitive compounds are packaged with gel ice packs and insulated liners to maintain cold chain integrity during transit. Products are shipped in sealed, light-protected vials to prevent degradation.

Unreconstituted (lyophilized) peptides should be stored in a freezer at -20°C for long-term storage. Once reconstituted, peptides should be refrigerated at 2-8°C and typically used within 30 days. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles, as this can degrade the compound.
